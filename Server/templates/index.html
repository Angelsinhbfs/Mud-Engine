Commands
Just enter text to say something to the room 
(L)ook [optional: Item/Person]: reads the description of the room/item/person.
(M)ove [Direction]: attempts to move in the stated direction.
    (N)orth, (E)ast, (S)outh, (W)est, (U)p, (D)own
(P)ick up [item name]: attempts to pick up the named item.
(A)ttack [(E)nemy name]: attacks the named enemy with your equipped weapon
(I)nventory: lists your inventory
(W)hisper [Player name] [Message]: attempts to send a message to the player named
